How can YOU a citizen help?


Doing these task all alone is not an easy job, thus we need your help:

SUPPORT CONSERVATION ORGANIZATION

Donate to or volunteer with organizations dedicated to wildlife conservation. Conservation organizations play a crucial role
in protecting endangered species and their habitats through a range of activities, including habitat preservation,
anti-poaching efforts, research, education, and policy advocacy. Financial donations help these organizations fund their projects,
hire staff, and purchase necessary equipment.




MAKE SUSTAINABLE CHOICES

Reduce your ecological footprint by consuming less, recycling, and supporting sustainable products. Individual choices can collectively
have a significant impact on the environment. By reducing consumption and waste, recycling materials, and choosing products
that are sustainably sourced, you can help lessen the demand for resources that lead to habitat destruction and pollution.
For example, choosing certified sustainable seafood helps protect marine ecosystems, while buying products made from recycled
materials reduces the need for raw materials.




EDUCATE OTHERS

Spread awareness about the importance of conserving wildlife and what individuals can do to help. Education is a powerful tool
for fostering a culture of conservation. By sharing information about the threats to wildlife and the steps people can take to mitigate
these threats, you can inspire others to take action. This can be done through various channels, including social media, community events,
educational programs, and personal conversations.



TOGETHER

Together with your help, we are able to protect these endangered animals from any more risks. When we think about it, most the causes of their
extinctions was because of us. And the sad part of all this, is that we have everything in our capabilities to stop harming them and their habitats.
Thus it is only morally right of us to take part all together and work in the preservation of these endangered animals and their habitats.
